Meeting Notes

Meeting 1 - Kick Off

Meeting Date: Tuesday, 28.03.2023 at 2:30 pm

Agenda:

  1. Discussion of planning progress
  2. Presentation and discussion of the current Literature Review Outline
  3. Discussion of the project focus
  4. Discussion of the further project development process
  5. Agreement of the next meeting

Meeting Notes:
1.

  • A first literature review was carried out.
  • There is a rough plan regarding the procedure.

2.
  • Some comments and suggested changes regarding the literature review outline are available in the form of feedback.
  • The subchapter "Topologies of smart home networks" is not necessary.
  • There should be less focus on communication protocols that have been established for a long time.

3.
  • It should be a less focus on techniques that have already been studied and discussed.
  • Greater focus on the questions: What is Matter? What makes Matter special? How is Matter different from other Smart Home IoT standards/protocols? What benefits can be seen in Matter? What dangers/threats can be identified?

4.
  • There is currently a lack of a reflected validation and verification process to justify how the goals in the project can be achieved, or how an appropriate evaluation process (hypothesis analysis) can be carried out.
  • An ethical discussion focusing on the responsibility of matter and possible consequences for users and society should be undertaken.
  • The creation of the research proposal is the next milestone. In addition to the formatives, which accompany the development process through feedback, another meeting is arranged before the deadline.

5.
  • Next meeting: Wednesday 12.04.2023 at 1:15 pm (GMT)
