  1. Presentation of the obtainded test results

Meeting 7 - Test results of the replay attack

Meeting Date: Thursday 13.07.2023 at 2:00 -2:30 pm (GMT)

Agenda:

  1. Presentation and discussion of the captured test results of the replay attack
  2. Confirmation of further development process of the project
  3. Agreement of the next meeting

Meeting Notes:
1.

  • The procedure for carrying out the replay attacks was presented. Approaches were also discussed which did not lead to any result.
  • It is noted that the practices that led to impasses can also be of great use as these should be mentioned under the lessons learned section of the discussion.
2.
  • The aspects of verification, validation and ethics must be taken into account in the dissertation. Verification refers to the question of how to ensure that the results of the tests are those that should be examined and are not influenced by other factors. Validation includes confirming the results with regard to the actual question. Ethics, in the context of this project, refers to the societal question of why Matter is necessary at all (in terms of interoperability and security), but also what meaning it has for the user and society.
  • It is recommended to support the introduction with known attacks and leaks in order to point out the need for a new communication standard in the smart home area.
  • The references of Github content should be based on the creator and the posting date.
  • A list of possible declaration formulations will be sent. The acknowledgments section will be discussed at a later date.
  • In terms of structure, the Results and discussion sections should be separated from each other. First of all, all test procedures and results should be presented and only then should a discussion of these take place.
