Collaborative Discussion 2

This Collaborative Discussion referred to a case study on accuracy of information. The researcher Abi is confronted with the challenge that the data collected about a product from his client could indicate that the product is harmful. A different choice of statistical evaluation methods can allow positive as well as negative conclusions. Therefore, the question of an appropriate procedure arises from an ethical as well as a legal point of view.
